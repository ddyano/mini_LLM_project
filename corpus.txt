it does not ask for permission
the way the morning light splits the oak tree
spilling liquid gold onto the silent pavement
we walk fast
counting minutes
carrying schedules like heavy winter coats
but the air is doing the hard work
holding up the birds
carrying the scent of rain from three miles away
binding the atoms of the world together without a single sound
to sit
just for a moment
is to witness the engineering of the quiet
it is enough
for now
just to watch the dust dance in the light
